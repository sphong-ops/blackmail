AB has length 1, so AB ∈ F with t = pi/3.

For interior C on AB to lie on no other F-segment, need x^(2/3)+y^(2/3) = 1 (on astroid envelope).

Line AB meets astroid at the tangent point: C = (cos^3(pi/3), sin^3(pi/3)) = (1/8, 3sqrt(3)/8).

Verify on AB: 2(1/8) + 2(3sqrt(3)/8)/sqrt(3) = 1/4 + 3/4 = 1. OK.
